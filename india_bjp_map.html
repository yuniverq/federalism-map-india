📊 Key Insights (2014-2019)

  • BJP Majority: States where BJP won clear majority on its own (shown with year BJP came to power)
  • BJP-led Coalition: States where BJP led coalition governments with alliance partners
  • Opposition: States where BJP was not in power or had negligible influence
  • Red dates: Indicate when BJP came to power in that state (only for BJP majority/coalition states)
  • Notable: Three states (MP, Rajasthan, Chhattisgarh) switched from BJP to INC in December 2018
